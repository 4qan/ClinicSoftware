# Plan 02-03: Integration: Visit History on Patient Profile, Cross-Wiring, Polish

## Objective
Wire Plans 01 and 02 together: display visit history on patient profiles, ensure drug autocomplete is fully integrated into the medication entry flow, and polish the end-to-end clinical workflow.

## must_haves
- Patient profile shows all past visits in reverse chronological order
- Each visit displays date, clinical notes, and medication details
- Drug autocomplete in MedicationEntry uses the real useDrugSearch hook with sub-300ms performance
- Custom drugs from Settings appear in prescription autocomplete
- "New Visit" button on patient profile navigates to New Visit with patient pre-selected

<task id="02-03-01" title="VisitCard component">
<description>
Create `src/components/VisitCard.tsx`: displays a single visit's details.

Props:
- `visit: Visit`
- `medications: VisitMedication[]`
- `defaultExpanded?: boolean` (default: false)

Renders:
- **Collapsed view:** Date (formatted as "Mar 6, 2026 - 2:30 PM"), first line of clinical notes truncated to ~80 chars, medication count badge (e.g., "3 medications"). Click to expand.
- **Expanded view:** Full clinical notes (preserve line breaks with whitespace-pre-wrap), medication table (drug name, dosage, frequency, duration per row), rx notes if present.
- **Actions:** "Edit" link (navigates to /visit/:id/edit), "Delete" button with confirmation modal that calls deleteVisit() and refreshes the list.

Styling: white card with left border accent (e.g., blue-600 left border for visual separation). Consistent with existing card patterns.

<task id="02-03-05" title="End-to-end workflow polish and cross-wiring">
<description>
Final integration pass to ensure the complete workflow is smooth:

1. **New Visit from patient profile:** Verify clicking "New Visit" on PatientProfilePage navigates to `/visit/new?patientId=xxx`, patient loads automatically and section collapses.

2. **Visit history after save:** After saving a new visit, navigating to the patient profile shows the new visit at the top of the history list.

3. **Edit flow round-trip:** Clicking "Edit" on a VisitCard navigates to EditVisitPage, editing and saving returns to the patient profile with updated data.

4. **Delete flow:** Deleting a visit from VisitCard removes it from the list (re-fetch or optimistic update).

5. **Drug autocomplete integration:** On the New Visit page, typing a partial drug name in the medication entry field shows autocomplete suggestions including both pre-seeded and any custom drugs added in Settings.

6. **Navigation consistency:** Breadcrumbs on New Visit and Edit Visit pages correctly show the patient name and link back to the profile.

7. **Edge cases:** Handle patient not found (invalid patientId in URL), visit not found (invalid visitId), network-independent operation (all IndexedDB).

Fix any integration issues discovered during this pass. This task is a sweep, not new feature work.
</description>
